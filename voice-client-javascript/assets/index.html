Now that your code is deployed, here are the last steps you need to do to finish your app.

  1. Log in to the admin page and initialize your environment
  2. Navigate to the browser page and make a call
  3. After the Client has connected, use your phone to call your TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER and your browser will ring
