Get started with your application

This app tracks and sends SMS alerts of email open and click statuses.

Follow these steps to try out your new app:

  1. Copy the following Webhook URL of your deployed template:
  2. Navigate and login to your SendGrid dashboard.
  3. Head to the Mail Settings section under the settings drop down.
  4. Under the Event Settings section, click on Event Webhook.
  5. Click Create new webhook.
  6. Enter your webhook URL from above within the Post URL textbox.
  7. Within the Engagement Data section, click the Opened and Clicked checkboxes. This will enable webhook event tracking for only those two events.

  8. Send an email out using SendGrid. You can use the categories field so that the SMS alert will show which category the email event is from.

Troubleshooting

  • - Verify the webhook URL is correct and points to your deployed function
  • - Ensure "Opened" and "Clicked" events are enabled in the Engagement Data section
  • - Check that the webhook endpoint is enabled (toggle should be on)
  • - Check your Twilio console for any error logs
  • - Verify your Twilio phone number and account credentials are properly configured
  • - Ensure the recipient phone number is in the correct format (E.164)
  • - Test your webhook by sending a test email and checking if events are being received at your endpoint
