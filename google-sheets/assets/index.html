Get started with your application

Now that your code is deployed, here are the last steps you need to do to finish your app.

This app automatically logs text messages sent to your Twilio phone number into the specified Google Sheets document.

  1. Open the Google Sheets document whose ID you configured for use with this app.
  2. Click the Share button in the toolbar of your Google Sheets document, enter the email address belonging to your service account , and press Enter to give your service account access to your Google Sheets document.
  3. Text your Twilio phone number with a message.
  4. Your spreadsheet should now contain the SID, phone number, and content of the text you sent.
