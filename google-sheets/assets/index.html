Configuring your application

Adding your Google Service account authentication JSON file:

Before you can use this application, you need to provide your Google Service account authentication JSON file. This file contains the credentials needed to access your Google Sheets document. Follow these steps to add your authentication JSON file to your application:

  1. Select Edit this application below to open the Functions and Assets Editor.
  2. Under Assets, select the /auth.json file.
  3. Paste the contents of your Google Service account authentication JSON key file into the /auth.json file.
  4. Select Save.
  5. Select Deploy all to redeploy your application.

For more information on how to obtain your authentication JSON file, refer to the Google Cloud documentation.

Get started with your application

Follow these steps to try out your new app:

This app automatically logs text messages sent to your Twilio phone number into the specified Google Sheets document.

  1. Go to the Google Sheets API setting in your Google Cloud Console and ensure it's enabled.
  2. Open the Google Sheets document whose ID you configured for use with this app.
  3. Click the Share button in the toolbar of your Google Sheets document, enter the email address belonging to your service account and press Enter to give your service account access to your Google Sheets document.
  4. Text your Twilio phone number with a message.
  5. Your spreadsheet should now contain the SID, phone number, and content of the text you sent.

Troubleshooting

Verify your Google Sheets configuration

Use the Verify Configuration button below to test your Google Sheets configuration.

If you encounter configuration issues, edit this function's .env file via the Edit this application button above, if available, and ensure that all values are correct. Some common error messages include:

  • Google Sheets integration error: The caller does not have permission: ensure you have shared your Google Sheets document with the email address associated with your Service Account.
  • Google Sheets integration error. Please check the debugger in your Twilio Console: ensure that you have entered the name of the Sheet within the Google Sheet document in the SHEET_NAME environment variable.

If you continue to experience issues, please refer to the debugger in your Twilio Console.
